Super Smash Bros. 4

An intense fighting game created for the 3DS & Wii U

About

Put your reflexes to the test in this hit release of Super Smash Bros.! This game can form AND destroy relationships simultaneously, depending on your gameplay style.

A good understanding of the controls is a MUST-HAVE if you wish to come out victorious.

Both versions have received positive reviews; critics applauded the fine-tuning of existing gameplay elements but criticized some issues with online play.

Release Date

The 3DS version was released in North America on October 3rd, 2014, while the Wii U version was released in North America on November 21st, 2014

How-To

Just jump right into the action! You'll get used to the controls and mechanics as time passes.

There's a huge learning curve to the game for newcomers. Once the hurdle is surpassed, though, the curve flattens out & becomes very minimal.
